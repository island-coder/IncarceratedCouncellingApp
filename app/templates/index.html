Created for a purpose

We believe that everyone, no matter their current circumstances, should have access to quality mental health care. That’s why we have created this platform to connect incarcerated individuals with licensed mental health professionals who are committed to helping you improve your mental health and well-being.

We've got the best..See for yourself.

Our platform is easy to use and completely confidential. Simply sign up and create an account, and you will be matched with a licensed mental health professionals who specialize in working with incarcerated individuals. Our mental health professionals have experience helping individuals deal with a range of mental health issues, including depression, anxiety, PTSD, addiction, and more..

With your interests in mind

We understand that privacy is of the utmost importance, especially when dealing with sensitive mental health issues. That’s why all communication between you and your mental health professional is completely confidential and secure. You can rest assured that your personal information and conversations will be kept private and only shared with your mental health professional.
